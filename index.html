Our Story

More than a school — a community of language lovers.

PrimeLanguages was founded on a simple belief: that learning a language should feel as natural as making a friend. For over 15 years, we've helped thousands of students — from 5-year-olds to 75-year-olds — find their voice in a new language.

Our teachers are native speakers, certified linguists, and passionate storytellers. Our classrooms are small, warm, and full of laughter. And our curriculum blends the best of communicative, immersive, and task-based methods so you don't just learn a language — you live it.

Our Method

How we get you speaking — fast.

Our 4-pillar approach blends the best of modern language science with the warmth of a real classroom. Every lesson is designed around one goal: getting you to communicate.

01

Immersive from Day One

Classes are taught 90%+ in the target language. You'll be ordering coffee in French by week two — we promise.

02

Communicative Focus

Forget endless grammar drills. We prioritize real conversations, role-plays, and scenarios you'll actually use.

03

Cultural Context

Language lives inside culture. Every lesson weaves in food, music, film, and traditions from native-speaking countries.

04

Spaced Repetition

Our curriculum uses science-backed review cycles so vocabulary sticks — and you actually retain what you learn.

Questions?

Everything you need to know.

Can't find what you're looking for? Our team is happy to help — just send us a message.

Contact Us →
Do I need any prior experience to join?
Not at all! We offer true beginner classes for every language. You'll start from zero and build up naturally.
How long does it take to become fluent?
It depends on the language and your goals. On average, students reach conversational level (B1) in 6–9 months with our Accelerator plan.
Are classes in-person, online, or both?
Both! We offer in-person classes at our center, live online classes, and hybrid options. You can switch between them anytime.
What's the class size?
Our group classes have a maximum of 8 students (6 for kids). This ensures plenty of speaking time and personal attention.
Do you offer exam preparation?
Yes! We prepare students for Cambridge (IELTS, FCE, CAE), DELE, DELF/DALF, Goethe, JLPT, HSK, and TOPIK exams.
Can I pause or cancel my subscription?
Absolutely. All our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Pause or cancel anytime from your dashboard.
